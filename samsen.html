Information

- Samsenwittayalai School education commitment for excellence in science, math, language skills internationally.

- The school flag, pink-colored flag at half the parcels. the length of the banner pink on green silk embroidered with a school badge below the left corner of the banner on the pink.

- The symbol consists of a beacon above an abbreviation of the school inside a circle. The mark also consists of a philosophy with the meaning of that philosophy written around the circle. The name of the school is on a green ribbon under the circle.

- In Samsenwittayalai School, classroom was divided into 5 different colors to give the students love and unity, each color represents a period of time in the history of Thailand : purple(Chiang Saen), yellow(Sukhothai), blue(Uthong), red(Ayutthaya),and green(Rattanakosin).
